The Beginning of a Vision

How One Insight Transformed the Industry

Our founder, Leon d'Ancona, recognized early on that understanding market trends and assessing agent performance were key to success in real estate. During a challenging period in the industry, Leon used data to identify high-performing agents and predict market fluctuations, which helped his business thrive when others struggled.

This insight led him to develop a solution that gave real estate professionals the power to make data-driven decisions. His innovative approach quickly gained attention, and within a year, RE/MAX awarded IMS Incorporated its largest statistics contract in their history.

What began as a tool for one business has since grown into REality Software, empowering brokers, agents, and multi-office firms with the data and insights they need to succeed in today’s competitive market.
